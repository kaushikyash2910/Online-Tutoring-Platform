Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common queries about courses, enrollment, pricing, and our policies.

We provide topic-specific short courses, comprehensive revision packages, and full-length courses for competitive exams (JEE/NEET). Each course includes recorded lectures, curated practice sets, and periodic live doubt sessions. Course pages list exact contents and assessment schedules.

Click Enroll on the course card, provide your contact details, and choose a payment option. After successful payment (or on COD confirmation), you will receive access credentials and an onboarding mail with next steps.

Refund requests are processed within 7 business days from purchase, subject to the refund terms described on the payment screen and the policy page. Partial refunds may apply to used content or consumed live classes; contact support for assistance.

Yes—selected courses provide completion certificates upon meeting attendance and assessment criteria. Certificates are verifiable and downloadable from your account dashboard.

For technical issues or account help, email support@quicktutor.example. For partnership enquiries, use partner@quicktutor.example. Response times are typically within 24–48 business hours.

We use regular diagnostic tests and activity logs to build a learning profile. The profile includes strengths, weaknesses, speed of solving, and topic readiness. Tutors receive this data to craft targeted assignments. Students can view progress charts in their dashboard and download weekly performance reports.

Our analytics also recommend revision windows and highlight topics that require additional practice, ensuring study time is efficiently allocated.
