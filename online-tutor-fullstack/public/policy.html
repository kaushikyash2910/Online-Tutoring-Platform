Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Clear, user-friendly summary of how we protect your data and what we expect from users.

Privacy policy — brief

We collect minimal personal data required to provide the service: name, email, course enrollments, and payment records. Data is stored securely and used to provide course access and improve learning outcomes. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Data access & deletion

Users can request a copy of their data, or request deletion by contacting support. We will verify ownership before honoring deletion requests.

Terms of use — short

By using QuickTutor, you agree not to redistribute paid content, to follow community guidelines, and to comply with applicable laws. Intellectual property for course material remains with QuickTutor and/or the authoring tutors.

Refunds & billing

Refunds are handled per course terms; some courses may have trial periods. Billing records are maintained for legal and compliance reasons.
